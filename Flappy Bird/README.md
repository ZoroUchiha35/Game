# Gameplay Overview 
This Flappy Bird game captures the classic challenge with a clean, modern look.
- **Objective:** Control the bird to fly between green pipes without hitting them or the ground. Each pipe pair you pass increases your **score** by one.
- **Controls:** Tap or click the **Game canvas**, or press the **Spacebar**, to make the bird flap and gain altitude. The game starts on your first input.
- **Progressive Speed:** The game speed starts at 1.0x. It increases by 0.2x every 5 points you score
